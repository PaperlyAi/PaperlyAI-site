🔒 Privacy by design

Your documents stay yours

Most scanner apps require an account, upload your documents to their cloud library, and use them to improve their AI. Paperly AI does none of that.

✓

No account, no ads

No sign-up, no email, no tracking. The only identifier sent is a random per-install token used to prevent abuse — no name, no phone, no device ID.

✓

Stored on your device

Every scanned document lives in the app's private storage on your phone — not in our cloud library. Delete the app and everything's gone.

✓

AI never trains on your scans

Text extraction runs on Google Gemini's paid tier, where Google contractually does not use your documents to train its models. Auto-deleted within 55 days.
